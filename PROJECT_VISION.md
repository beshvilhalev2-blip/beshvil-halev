# Beshvil HaLev — Project Vision

## Identity

Beshvil HaLev is not a travel blog.

It is becoming a family outdoor companion platform for Israel.

## Brand Motto

להיות בתנועה זאת התרופה

## Mission

Help people gain confidence and leave home to experience nature, regardless of family status, budget, experience level, or vehicle.

## Core Philosophy

The platform's job is not to publish more routes.

Its job is to reduce friction between intention and motion.

Help users move from:

"אולי נצא לטייל"

to:

"אנחנו יוצאים לטייל"

## Product Pillars

1. Find My Trip
2. Trip Pages
3. Gear Center
4. Want To Travel / בא לי לטייל
5. Family Outdoor Planning
6. Trust, simplicity and accessibility

## UX Principles

- Mobile first
- RTL Hebrew
- Calm, not overwhelming
- Confidence over excitement
- Practical information before long content
- Simplicity over feature overload
- No aggressive ads
- No clutter
- Help users make decisions quickly

## Design Direction

Airbnb + Apple + Patagonia

The website should feel:

- warm
- clean
- premium
- trustworthy
- family-friendly
- nature-first
- modern
- easy to return to

## Approved Future Concepts

- Milana & Kids verified badge
- Family Suitability Score
- Trip Decision Pages
- Interactive Israel Map
- Live Conditions Layer
- Milana AI
- Community and newsletter
- Travel benefits and partnerships
- Movement reminders / תזכורת תנועה

## Current Priority Order

1. Build the content and photo library
2. Add real photography
3. Improve trip pages into decision pages
4. Add Family Suitability Score
5. Add verified badge
6. Plan the interactive map

## Product Decision Rule

Before suggesting or building any feature, ask:

"Does this help users leave home and go outdoors?"

If the answer is no, it is not a priority.
